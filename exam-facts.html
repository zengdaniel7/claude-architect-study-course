✅ Exam Facts — Official vs Reported

Last checked 2026-06-24 against official Anthropic sources.

Read this once, then relax. Most of the exact exam numbers online are NOT published by Anthropic. They're repeated on study-prep sites and may be right — treat them as a study target, not gospel. Your learning plan doesn't change.

✅ Officially confirmed

  • The certification is real: "Claude Certified Architect, Foundations" — Anthropic's first technical certification, for "solution architects building production applications with Claude."
  • It launched with the Claude Partner Network (announced March 2026).
  • Registration is partner-gated. Access is through the Partner Network / Partner Portal — not open public sign-up as of mid-2026.
  • It's a paid exam. Partner-company employees get discounts / free early-access seats. Network membership is free; the exam is not free in general.
  • Prep = Anthropic Academy (Anthropic's own courses, on Skilljar) — see Resources.
  • Anthropic does not publish a public exam blueprint page.

🚪 What this means for YOU

You're a solo beginner, and the exam is currently partner-gated. So:
  1. Learning now = 100% worth it. The skills, courses, and projects are real and free to access. That's the prize.
  2. Sitting the exam = a separate logistics question. Before booking, check: is your (future) employer a Claude Partner? Email academy-support@anthropic.com about individual eligibility + the official exam guide.
  3. Don't pay third-party sites for "official" practice exams. Get the real guide from Anthropic first.

⚠️ Where exam-prep wording ≠ official docs

  • "isRetryable" error field (seen in prep quizzes): there is no official isRetryable field. The real mechanism is is_error: true (Anthropic) / isError: true (MCP) plus a clear, instructive error message. Learn the real one; recognise the prep wording.
  • Structured output is stronger than older guides say: official Structured Outputs use constrained decoding to guarantee schema-valid JSON.
