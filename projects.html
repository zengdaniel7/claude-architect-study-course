🔨 Project Ladder & Rubrics

Each project turns exam vocabulary into something you can see, explain, and defend. Do them in order.

📊 Grading levels (use on every project)

  • Beginner — you can name parts but can't explain tradeoffs yet.
  • Developing — you can explain the normal path but miss failures.
  • Exam-ready — normal path, failures, and why your design beats alternatives.
  • Strong — you can teach it visually and adapt it to a new scenario.
